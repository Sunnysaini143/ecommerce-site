Why Visit Mussoorie

It offers a wonderful view of the Himalayan snow ranges to the north-east and the Doon Valley. Captivating paradise for leisure travelers and honeymooners, it is a perfect summer resort. Mussoorie is spread across a height of 2,000 m above sea level. From this vantage point, it offers scenic views of the Himalayan peaks in western Garhwal.
